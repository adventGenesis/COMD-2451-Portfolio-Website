WHO AM I?!?!?!?!?!

My name is Mekhi. I'm a college student at New York City College of Technology majoring in Communication Design. My dream is to become a freelance artist & animator, As well as potentially a graphic designer, and one day even make my own comic. When I’m not drowning in schoolwork, you’ll probably catch ne watching movies, playing video games, or listening to some music. And rarely, doodling in my sketchbook.


I mainly like drawing fanart of franchises that I like, and sometimes my own original characters.
